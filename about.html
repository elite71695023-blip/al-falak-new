Who We Are

Al Falak Transport LLC is a Dubai-based heavy equipment rental and logistics company operating across all seven Emirates. We serve construction, infrastructure, oil & gas, and industrial clients who demand more than just a machine on-site.

Based at Dubai Investment Park 2, our fleet spans 14 equipment categories — from 250-tonne mobile cranes to compact spider lifts — all RTA-certified, maintained to the highest standard, and supported by our in-house operations team 24/7.

Our difference is simple: we provide the operational backbone, not just the iron. Every deployment is backed by certified operators, logistics planning, and round-the-clock support — because on your site, downtime is never an option.

The Mission
Al Falak was founded on a single conviction: that Dubai's construction industry deserved a heavy equipment partner that operated with the same precision and accountability expected on the sites it served. Not just machines for rent — a full operational capability that project managers could rely on from mobilization day to project completion.
The Fleet Philosophy
Our fleet is built around versatility and specialization in equal measure. From 250-tonne all-terrain mobile cranes capable of major infrastructure lifts, to sub-one-tonne spider cranes that can ride elevators to upper floors — we invest in equipment that solves the problems other companies say are impossible. Every unit is RTA-certified, third-party inspected, and maintained to manufacturer standards by our in-house team.
The People
Every operator in our team is RTA-certified, HSE safety inducted, and trained in defensive operation protocols specific to UAE site conditions. We don't sub out our operator supply — our team is ours, which means every engagement is backed by people who understand and uphold the Al Falak standard of zero harm and complete accountability.
The Commitment
Our 24/7 operations model means that when your site needs emergency recovery, unplanned fuel management, or same-day additional equipment, you reach a real person who can mobilize immediately. We're based in Dubai Investment Park 2, positioned for rapid response across all seven Emirates. That's not a marketing line — it's the reason our clients call us back project after project.
